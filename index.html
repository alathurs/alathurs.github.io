ABOUT

Sreejith Alathur is a faculty of Information Systems. His research focuses on security aspects of eParticipation and Social media. He did his PhD in the areas of eGovernance from Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IITD). His thesis has been chosen as a Highly Commended Emerald/EFMD Outstanding Doctoral Research Award.

Dr. Alathur completed his MBA and M.Tech in Software Engineering. He has his B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Cochin University of Science and Technology , India. He received research scholarship from International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad for a project on Formal Ontology.

Dr. Alathur is the recipient of the 2014 National Internet Exchange of India (NIXI) fellowship. His publications have appeared in the areas of cyber security, data science, education, health, disaster & disability studies. His publications listed under Most cited, downloaded, and received the best paper awards.

He served as convener for accessibility audit and works on Internet protocols. He received various research grants and successfully supervised five doctoral students. He thought undergraduate and postgraduate modules on Information Systems and Business Analytics. He currently teaches system thinking and conceptual modeling.

Work Experience

Dr.Alathur, previously taught at Department of Computer Science and Engineering. After graduation he was part of various Information System and biological modeling projects. During his post graduate days he associated with Ontology development projects.
